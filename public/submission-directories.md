## Quick-Start Strategy

**Week 1:** Submit to Tier 1 + Tier 2 (15 directories)
**Weeks 2–4:** Submit to Tier 3 at 3–4 per week
**Weeks 5–8:** Submit to Tier 4 at 2–3 per week

Use the product description from `public/product-description.txt` and screenshots from `public/screenshots/` as your reusable listing kit.
