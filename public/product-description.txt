# Spire

**Tagline:** AI-powered SOC 2 and EU AI Act compliance automation for B2B SaaS.

**One-liner:** Spire connects to your infrastructure, continuously collects audit evidence across 30 integrations via Composio, runs an on-demand AI compliance audit against SOC 2 + EU AI Act controls (pass/fail verdicts per control), and auto-fills enterprise security questionnaires in minutes — so you stop losing deals to procurement delays.

Spire is an AI-driven compliance platform that replaces manual, spreadsheet-driven audit prep with continuous evidence collection, an AI agent that actually runs on-demand control audits (not just collects data), and AI questionnaire auto-fill. Built for B2B SaaS companies selling to enterprise, it closes three compliance bottlenecks: evidence gathering, ongoing audit readiness, and security questionnaire responses.

**How it works:**

Spire connects via read-only OAuth (no agents, no sidecars) through Composio to the infrastructure your engineering team already uses — AWS, GitHub, Stripe, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, GitLab, Jira, Linear, Okta, Datadog, Sentry, PagerDuty, Snyk, Salesforce, HubSpot, and 15 more. Each integration's API is probed automatically and the results are mapped to specific SOC 2 trust service criteria and EU AI Act articles. For example, the GitHub integration surfaces branch protection rules (CC6.1), pull request review requirements (CC6.1), secret scanning alerts (CC7.2), and membership changes (CC6.3). All evidence is timestamped and stored in an auditor-ready library.

**AI Compliance Agent:**

Unlike traditional platforms that only collect evidence, Spire includes an AI agent that actually performs on-demand audit readiness assessments. It evaluates the collected evidence against all 56 SOC 2 Common Criteria and 10 EU AI Act articles (4, 50, 52, 53, 55 plus high-risk-classification rules), producing a structured audit report with pass/fail/warning verdicts per control, evidence citations, and remediation recommendations. The audit streams live as NDJSON, so your team can watch each verdict land.

**Security Questionnaire Automation:**

Spire uses its compliance knowledge base to auto-fill enterprise security questionnaires. Upload any format (PDF, DOCX, CSV, Markdown, web form) and the AI maps each question to relevant evidence and generates a response. Each answer carries a 0–100% confidence score; only uncertain ones need human review. A 200-question enterprise review typically drops from ~40 engineering hours of draft-and-check to a few hours of confirmed-review.

**Audit-Ready Export:** Generate structured evidence packs for SOC 2 and EU AI Act in one click. Organized by control, timestamped, grouped by framework — ready for auditor handoff.

**Supported frameworks:** SOC 2 (all five trust service criteria), EU AI Act (Articles 4, 50, plus high-risk-classification rules, GPAI Code), ISO 42001 (on roadmap).
