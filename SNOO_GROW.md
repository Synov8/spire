## Product Profile

**Project Name:** Spire

**Product Description:**
Spire is an autonomous AI compliance agent for B2B SaaS companies. It connects to your engineering and infrastructure tools (GitHub, Stripe, Cloudflare, etc.) via read-only OAuth, then runs continuous audit checks against SOC 2 and EU AI Act controls. It streams every tool call and evaluation in real time so you can see exactly how each conclusion was reached. When enterprise buyers send security questionnaires, Spire investigates your live infrastructure to draft answers with evidence citations and confidence scores. It replaces the manual spreadsheet-and-screenshot grind that founders hate. No consultants. No security team required.

**Target Audience:**
CTOs, founders, and engineering leads at B2B SaaS companies (5-150 employees) who need SOC 2 compliance to close enterprise deals but don't have a dedicated compliance or security team.

**Problems Solved:**
- SOC 2 prep taking 3-6 months of manual evidence gathering
- Losing enterprise deals because security questionnaires go unanswered
- Engineering teams spending weeks filling out vendor security reviews instead of shipping product
- No visibility into whether you'd pass an audit until the auditor shows up
- Growing AI features creating undocumented compliance risk

**Keywords:**
SOC 2, compliance, security questionnaire, enterprise sales, EU AI Act, startup compliance, security review, Vanta, Drata, audit readiness

**Key Features:**
- Autonomous AI audit agent that streams results in real time
- 30+ read-only OAuth integrations (GitHub, Stripe, Cloudflare, Neon, etc.)
- Questionnaire auto-fill with per-question confidence scores
- Downloadable PDF compliance reports
- Manual evidence upload with batch re-evaluation
