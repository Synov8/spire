Meeting security questionnaire response SLAs consistently requires a structured management process, not just a calendar reminder — and companies that implement formal SLA tracking report 90%+ on-time response rates compared to 60% for those without, according to a 2025 Gartner procurement operations study. SLA management should be integrated into your questionnaire response workflow, not treated as a separate tracking exercise.

## What SLA Targets Should You Set?

Initial acknowledgment: 24 hours (auto-acknowledgment counts). Standard questionnaire completion: 5 to 10 business days. Complex custom questionnaire: 10 to 15 business days. Follow-up question response: 48 hours. Emergency requests: 24 to 48 hours with executive approval. Define these targets in your trust center and sales playbook.

## How Do You Track SLA Performance?

Track with a questionnaire management platform that includes SLA timer for each active questionnaire, automated escalation when SLA exceeds 75% of target without completion, weekly SLA compliance report showing on-time percentage by team member, and trend reporting showing SLA compliance over time.

## What Happens When an SLA Is at Risk?

When a questionnaire is at risk of missing its SLA deadline: escalate to the questionnaire owner's manager, offer the prospect a preliminary response for critical questions with a commitment to complete remaining questions within an extended timeline, and prioritize resources to complete the most time-sensitive questionnaires.

## FAQ

### Should I include questionnaire SLAs in customer contracts?

Avoid including specific questionnaire response SLAs in contracts — they create contractual liability for administrative processes. Instead, communicate response SLAs as your standard service commitment without contractual obligation.

### How do you handle prospects who submit multiple questionnaires simultaneously?

Prioritize based on deal stage and close date. Communicate the prioritization to the prospect and commit to completing all questionnaires within an extended total timeline.

### What is a reasonable SLA extension request?

Request a 5 to 10 business day extension if you need more time. Communicate the reason for the delay and the new committed completion date. Most procurement teams accept one reasonable extension.
