Companies that track compliance metrics and KPIs — and report them to leadership quarterly — maintain control effectiveness rates of 95% or higher compared to approximately 82% for companies without formal compliance measurement, according to a 2025 AICPA compliance monitoring study. The right metrics turn compliance from a subjective assessment into a data-driven program.

## What Control Effectiveness Metrics Should You Track?

Control pass rate (percentage of controls passing evidence review — target 95%+), evidence collection completeness (percentage of scheduled evidence collections completed on time — target 98%+), automated evidence coverage (percentage of controls with automated evidence — target 70% to 80%), and control drift frequency (number of times a previously passing control fails — track trend, zero tolerance for recurring drifts).

## What Operational Compliance Metrics Matter?

Remediation mean time to close (average days from finding identification to resolution — target 30 days or less), vendor assessment completion rate (percentage of in-scope vendor assessments completed within the review period — target 100%), employee training completion rate (percentage of employees completing annual security training — target 95% within 30 days, 100% within 60 days), and access review on-time completion (percentage of access reviews completed within the scheduled review window — target 100%).

## What Audit-Related Metrics Should You Track?

Audit finding recurrence (percentage of previous-year findings that recur — target 0%), auditor evidence request response time (average days to respond to auditor evidence requests — target 48 hours), audit fieldwork duration (actual days vs planned — target within 10% of plan), and findings by severity (critical, high, medium, low — target zero critical findings).

## FAQ

### How should compliance metrics be reported?

Quarterly compliance dashboard to leadership: control effectiveness scorecard, remediation progress, vendor risk posture, and audit readiness status. Board-level metrics should focus on strategic compliance health, not operational details.

### What is a good control pass rate for SOC 2?

95% or higher overall pass rate. Individual controls should have documented remediation plans if pass rate drops below 90%. Any control below 80% requires immediate investigation.

### How do you track evidence collection timeliness?

Most compliance platforms track evidence collection timeliness automatically. Set scheduled collection runs with success/failure monitoring. Missed collections should generate alerts with defined escalation.
