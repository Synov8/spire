Healthcare buyers — hospitals, health systems, digital health platforms, and health insurers — require HIPAA compliance evidence that goes beyond SOC 2, and 82% of healthcare RFPs require HIPAA-specific questionnaire responses plus a signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA) before contract execution, according to a 2025 Healthcare Compliance Association procurement study. Healthcare security reviews are the most document-intensive outside of government.

## What Healthcare Regulations Affect Security Questionnaires?

HIPAA Privacy Rule governs use and disclosure of Protected Health Information (PHI). HIPAA Security Rule requires administrative, physical, and technical safeguards for electronic PHI (ePHI). HITECH Act strengthened HIPAA enforcement and added breach notification requirements. HITRUST CSF provides a certifiable framework that many healthcare organizations require from vendors. State-specific healthcare privacy laws (California, Washington, Nevada) add additional requirements.

## What Do Healthcare Questionnaires Ask?

Healthcare questionnaires focus on: ePHI identification and data flow mapping, encryption of ePHI at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+), access controls for ePHI with role-based restrictions, business associate agreements with all subcontractors, breach notification procedures with 60-day regulatory notification capability, and HITRUST certification level (if applicable).

## How Should You Respond to Healthcare Questionnaires?

Maintain healthcare-specific response materials including HIPAA compliance documentation (if applicable), SOC 2 Type II report (Security + Privacy TSC preferred), signed BAA template ready for customer signature, ePHI data flow diagram showing where PHI is stored and transmitted, and HITRUST certification report (if certified).

## FAQ

### Can SOC 2 replace HIPAA compliance for healthcare buyers?

No. SOC 2 does not replace HIPAA compliance. HIPAA has specific requirements — BAAs, NPI disclosure accounting, patient rights — that SOC 2 does not cover. SOC 2 can satisfy a subset of HIPAA Security Rule requirements.

### What is the minimum HITRUST level healthcare buyers accept?

HITRUST r2 Certified (Level 2) is the most common requirement for healthcare vendor compliance. Some large health systems require HITRUST r2 Validated (Level 3) for vendors with broad ePHI access.

### How long does it take to complete a healthcare security questionnaire?

80 to 200 questions, typically requiring 2 to 4 weeks for full completion. Have healthcare-specific response materials prepared before entering the sales cycle.
