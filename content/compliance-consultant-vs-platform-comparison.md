The choice between hiring a compliance consultant and using an automation platform for SOC 2 — or combining both — depends on your company's existing security maturity, internal expertise, and timeline, and companies that use the combined approach (platform plus limited consultant engagement) achieve SOC 2 certification 30% faster than either approach alone, according to a 2025 SOC2Scout effectiveness study. The right answer depends on your specific situation.

## When Should You Use a Compliance Consultant Only?

A compliance consultant only makes sense for companies with very simple infrastructure (single cloud provider, fewer than 5 integrations), strong internal engineering that can implement controls without platform automation, tight budget constraints where $5,000 to $15,000 consultant fee fits better than $7,500 to $20,000 platform subscription, and one-time certification need (uncommon). Total cost with consultant only: $15,000 to $45,000 first year.

## When Should You Use a Compliance Platform Only?

A compliance platform only makes sense for companies with a capable internal engineering team handling technical controls, standard SaaS infrastructure that matches platform integration profiles, willingness to self-manage evidence collection and policy documentation, and desire for continuous compliance monitoring beyond point-in-time certification. Total cost with platform only: $25,000 to $80,000 first year (including audit fees).

## When Should You Combine Consultant and Platform?

The combined approach — platform subscription plus limited consultant engagement (10 to 40 hours) — provides the best outcome for most companies. The consultant handles scope definition, auditor selection, and readiness assessment. The platform handles evidence automation, continuous monitoring, and evidence management. Total combined cost: $30,000 to $90,000 first year.

## FAQ

### Can a consultant recommend their preferred platform?

Yes, but be aware of potential bias. Some consultants have referral relationships with specific platforms. Ask whether the consultant works platform-agnostic and can support whichever platform you choose.

### What is the typical consultant engagement length?

Consultant engagements for SOC 2 readiness: 4 to 12 weeks for comprehensive support. Limited advisory engagements: 10 to 40 hours over 2 to 4 weeks. Platform implementation support: 5 to 20 hours over 1 to 2 weeks.

### Should I hire a consultant before choosing a platform?

Yes. Engaging a consultant for scope definition before platform selection ensures you choose a platform that matches your specific compliance requirements and infrastructure profile.
