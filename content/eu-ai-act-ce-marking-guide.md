CE marking under the EU AI Act signals that a high-risk AI system meets all applicable requirements — and companies that coordinate AI Act CE marking with existing CE marking obligations (MDR, Machinery Directive, Radio Equipment Directive) reduce certification overhead by 30% to 50%, according to a 2026 EU Commission CE marking coordination guide. CE marking is the final step after completing conformity assessment.

## What Does CE Marking Under the AI Act Require?

CE marking requires: completed conformity assessment (internal or notified body), issued EU declaration of conformity (DoC) per Annex V, technical documentation per Annex IV, quality management system per Article 17, and registration in the EU-wide AI database per Article 60. The CE marking must be visible, legible, and indelible on the AI system or its packaging and documentation.

## What Must the EU Declaration of Conformity Include?

The DoC must include: AI system name, type, model, and batch or serial number, provider name and address, statement of sole responsibility for conformity, object of the declaration (system description sufficient for traceability), AI Act and other applicable EU harmonization legislation references, conformity assessment procedure used, notified body name and number (if applicable), declaration date and signatory information.

## How Does AI CE Marking Interact With Other CE Marking?

AI systems that are also medical devices (MDR), machinery (Machinery Directive), or radio equipment (RED) require CE marking for each applicable directive. The AI Act's CE marking should be accompanied by CE markings for the other applicable legislation. The documentation should reference all applicable legislation.

## FAQ

### Is CE marking required for all AI systems?

CE marking is required only for high-risk AI systems. Limited-risk and minimal-risk AI systems do not require CE marking. General-purpose AI models have separate transparency obligations but do not require CE marking.

### Can I affix CE marking before completing conformity assessment?

No. CE marking must only be affixed after completing the full conformity assessment and issuing the declaration of conformity. False CE marking is a compliance violation subject to penalties.

### How long is CE marking valid for an AI system?

CE marking is valid as long as the AI system remains in compliance with the AI Act requirements. Significant changes to the AI system — new training data, changed purpose, architecture updates — may require re-assessment and updated CE marking.
