Effective security questionnaire delegation — assigning each question to the right team member by topic area — reduces overall response time by 40% while improving answer accuracy, and companies with formal delegation workflows complete questionnaires in 5 days average compared to 14 days for those without, according to a 2025 procurement cycle time analysis. The key is having clear routing rules that eliminate back-and-forth.

## What Question Topics Map to Which Teams?

Technical questions (encryption standards, network security, access controls, vulnerability management) route to the security or engineering team. Policy and process questions (incident response, business continuity, training, vendor management) route to compliance or GRC team. Legal and contractual questions (data processing agreements, liability, regulatory compliance) route to legal team.

Sales-appropriate questions (company overview, certifications, customer references, roadmap) route to sales or security sales engineering. Data privacy questions (data retention, data subject rights, cross-border transfers) route to privacy officer or legal.

## How Do You Set Up Delegation Workflows?

Use a questionnaire management platform that supports automated question routing based on topic, collaborator assignment with deadlines, response review and approval workflows, version control and change tracking, and status tracking across all active questionnaires.

## What Is the Approval Workflow for Questionnaire Responses?

Standard workflow: assignee drafts response (24 to 48 hours), technical reviewer validates accuracy (24 hours), compliance reviewer ensures consistency with policies (24 hours), final approver (compliance lead or legal depending on sensitivity) signs off (24 hours). Total workflow: 4 to 8 business days.

## FAQ

### Should the sales team answer security questions directly?

Sales team members should answer only company-level questions (certifications held, coverage dates, customer references). Technical and compliance questions should route to the appropriate specialist. Untrained sales responses are a common source of inaccurate security claims.

### How do you handle questions that span multiple teams?

Assign a primary owner who coordinates with secondary contributors. The primary owner consolidates responses and resolves any contradictions between teams before submission.

### What if the engineer with the answer is unavailable?

Maintain written response guidance for each technical area so any team member can provide a preliminary answer. Document known technical configuration values in a shared knowledge base.
