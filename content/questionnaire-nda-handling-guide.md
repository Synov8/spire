NDA negotiation is one of the most common friction points in enterprise security review processes, and companies that streamline NDA handling — using pre-approved templates and clear signing authority — reduce security review cycle time by 3 to 7 days compared to those that negotiate NDAs per-deal, according to a 2025 procurement cycle time study. The key is having an NDA strategy before the first prospect asks.

## When Should You Sign a Prospect's NDA?

Sign the prospect's NDA when their standard NDA is mutual, reasonable, and similar to your standard terms. Sign when the deal size justifies the legal review investment. Sign when the prospect is in a regulated industry (financial services, healthcare, government) with specific NDA requirements. Push back when the NDA is one-sided (unilateral in their favor only), includes broad indemnification that goes beyond confidentiality, contains non-competition clauses, or requires perpetual confidentiality for non-trade-secret information.

## Should You Have a Standard NDA Template?

Yes. Having a pre-approved standard mutual NDA that you send to prospects saves 2 to 5 days per deal. Your standard NDA should be reviewed by legal counsel and approved for use without additional legal review for deals under a certain size threshold.

## What NDA Terms Affect SOC 2 Information Sharing?

The SOC 2 report contains detailed control descriptions and system information that many companies consider confidential. Ensure your NDA covers the SOC 2 report, the system description, and evidence artifacts shared during the review. Limit the NDA to confidentiality purposes only — not extended to liability, indemnification, or business terms.

## FAQ

### Can I share my SOC 2 report without an NDA?

Most companies require a signed NDA before sharing the full SOC 2 Type II report. The scope and system description contain sensitive infrastructure details. The SOC 3 report is designed for public distribution and does not require an NDA.

### How long does standard NDA negotiation take?

Standard mutual NDA: 1 to 3 business days if both parties use standard forms. Custom NDA negotiation: 1 to 3 weeks. Pre-approved templates reduce this to same-day exchange.

### Who should have authority to sign NDAs?

Define NDA signing authority by deal size: under $50K ACV — compliance manager or legal counsel can sign; over $50K ACV — requires VP-level approval. Clear authority prevents bottlenecks.
