Financial services buyers — banks, credit unions, insurance companies, and fintech platforms — subject vendors to the most rigorous security review process outside of government procurement, and 78% of financial services RFPs require evidence of alignment with FFIEC guidelines or NYDFS 500 requirements in addition to SOC 2, according to a 2025 Bank Security Association survey. Financial services questionnaire responses require specific regulatory knowledge.

## What Financial Regulations Affect Security Questionnaires?

FFIEC (Federal Financial Institutions Examination Council) guidelines apply to any vendor serving federally regulated financial institutions. The FFIEC CAT (Cybersecurity Assessment Tool) is commonly used. NYDFS 23 NYCRR Part 500 applies to vendors serving New York financial institutions and requires specific controls: MFA, encryption, incident response, and annual certification. GLBA (Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act) applies to vendors handling consumer financial information.

## What Do Financial Services Questionnaires Ask?

Financial questionnaires focus on: encryption standards for financial data at rest and in transit (AES-256 minimum), access control segregation between financial and non-financial systems, incident response notification timeline (typically 24 hours for financial data incidents), business continuity and disaster recovery testing frequency and results, subcontractor access to financial data, and regulatory examination history and results.

## How Do You Respond to Financial Services Questionnaires?

Maintain financial services-specific response materials including SOC 2 Type II report (non-negotiable for financial buyers), FFIEC CAT mapping showing SOC 2 control alignment, NYDFS 500 compliance statement (if applicable), penetration testing results within the last 12 months, and business continuity plan and DR test results.

## FAQ

### Do financial services buyers accept SOC 2 alone?

Large financial institutions generally require more than SOC 2 — they expect SOC 2 plus a completed financial services-specific questionnaire (often 200+ questions). Community banks may accept SOC 2 as sufficient.

### What is the typical financial services questionnaire length?

150 to 400 questions covering IT security, physical security, business continuity, vendor management, data privacy, and regulatory compliance. Expect 3 to 6 weeks for full completion.

### How do I prepare for financial services audit rights?

Financial services contracts typically include audit rights with 24 to 48 hours notice for cause. Be prepared for on-site or remote audits within the contract term. Maintain current evidence for all controls to support potential audits.
