Vendor risk assessment questionnaires (VRAs) are the gatekeeping mechanism that enterprise procurement teams use to evaluate SaaS vendors before contract signing. A 2025 Gartner analysis found that 58% of SaaS companies lose at least one enterprise deal per quarter due to incomplete or slow VRA responses, with average deal sizes of $75,000 to $250,000 ACV at stake per lost opportunity.

## What Is the Typical VRA Process?

The VRA process follows five stages: procurement triggers VRA request based on deal value and data sensitivity, vendor receives questionnaire containing 50 to 300 questions across security domains, vendor responds with answers and supporting evidence, procurement risk team evaluates responses against a scoring rubric, and vendor either passes (proceeds to contract) or fails (requires remediation plan or deal termination).

Understanding the scoring rubric is essential. Most enterprise VRAs use weighted scoring where SOC 2 certification, encryption, and access control questions carry the highest point values. Failing a high-weight question can fail the entire assessment even if all low-weight questions pass.

## What Industries Have the Strictest VRAs?

Financial services VRAs are the most demanding, requiring ISO 27001 certification in addition to SOC 2 Type II, annual penetration testing, dedicated compliance personnel, and 72-hour breach notification. Healthcare VRAs require HIPAA BAAs and HITRUST certification for covered entity data. Government sector VRAs require FedRAMP or StateRAMP authorization for any data processing.

## What Documentation Should You Prepare in Advance?

The advance documentation package should include your SOC 2 Type II report (current within 12 months), penetration testing executive summary (within 12 months), business continuity plan summary, data processing agreement, sub-processor list, insurance certificate with cybersecurity coverage, and organizational chart showing security reporting lines. Having these ready before the VRA arrives reduces response time by approximately 60%.

## How Do You Handle VRA Questions Beyond Your Current Controls?

Answer honestly with compensating controls and timelines. Enterprise buyers respect transparency and a defined remediation roadmap more than aspirational claims. A documented security roadmap with quarterly milestones demonstrates maturity even when specific controls are not yet implemented.

## FAQ

### Should we automate VRA responses?

Yes. Automation platforms that maintain answer libraries with SOC 2 evidence links reduce VRA response time from 40+ hours to 2-4 hours and achieve 60%+ auto-response rates.

### What is the acceptable VRA response time?

Enterprise buyers expect initial acknowledgment within 24 hours and complete response within 5 to 10 business days. Faster responses correlate with higher pass rates.
