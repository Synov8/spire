Choosing the wrong compliance consultant wastes time and money — a 2026 Gartner compliance services survey found that 34% of companies that hired a compliance consultant were dissatisfied with the outcome, with the most common complaint being that the consultant didn't understand their technology stack. This guide provides 25 questions to evaluate compliance consultants before hiring them for SOC 2 implementation.

## Experience and Track Record Questions

1. How many SOC 2 Type II certifications have you personally led? (Look for 10+)
2. What percentage of your clients pass their SOC 2 audit on the first attempt? (Target 90%+)
3. What industries do you specialize in? (Prefer experience in your specific vertical)
4. How many clients at our stage ($X ARR, X employees) have you worked with?
5. What was your most challenging SOC 2 engagement and how did you handle it?
6. Have you worked with our technology stack (mention specific tools)?

## Methodology and Approach Questions

7. Walk me through your SOC 2 implementation methodology from start to finish.
8. How do you prioritize controls during implementation?
9. What percentage of controls do you typically automate vs leave manual?
10. How do you handle scope definition and boundary decisions?
11. What is your approach to evidence collection and organization?
12. How do you prepare employees for auditor interviews?

## Technical Knowledge Questions

13. How do you configure CloudTrail for SOC 2 evidence collection?
14. What GitHub branch protection rules satisfy CC8.1?
15. How would you implement tenant isolation in a multi-tenant PostgreSQL database?
16. What is the difference between SOC 2 Type I and Type II control testing? (They should explain testing of operating effectiveness)
17. How do you map CIS controls to SOC 2 trust service criteria?

## Communication and Relationship Questions

18. How do you communicate compliance requirements to engineers without creating friction?
19. How often will we meet, and what is your availability during our audit?
20. How do you handle disagreements with auditors about control evidence?
21. Will you be our primary point of contact or will we work with junior team members?
22. How do you document knowledge transfer so we're not dependent on you?

## Pricing and Logistics Questions

23. What is your pricing model and what's included? (Get detailed scope of work)
24. What is your estimated timeline for our engagement?
25. Can you provide 3-5 references from companies similar to ours?
