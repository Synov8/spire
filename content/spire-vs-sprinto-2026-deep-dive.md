Spire and Sprinto offer SOC 2 automation with different geographic strengths and technical approaches in 2026 — Sprinto has strong adoption in India and APAC markets with automated evidence collection and a GRC-integrated platform, while Spire offers AI-native compliance with automated evidence evaluation and a global focus. Both platforms enable SOC 2 readiness in 6 to 12 weeks for most B2B SaaS companies.

## Geographic Coverage and Market Focus

Sprinto has particularly strong adoption among Indian SaaS companies and APAC-based startups, with specific support for local compliance frameworks. Spire serves a global customer base with support for US, EU, and APAC compliance requirements. Both platforms support SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR, and PCI DSS compliance.

## AI and Automation

Spire differentiates with its AI audit agent that automatically evaluates evidence and produces structured readiness reports. Sprinto provides automated evidence collection and policy management but without AI-powered evidence evaluation. Spire also offers AI-driven questionnaire response automation.

## Pricing

Sprinto pricing starts at approximately $6,000 to $12,000 per year for SOC 2 coverage, positioning it as a cost-effective option for early-stage startups. Spire pricing is competitive with similar platform tiers. Both platforms offer multi-framework support.

## Best-Fit Customer Profile

Spire is best for: global SaaS companies wanting AI-powered compliance automation, teams that want automated evidence evaluation, and companies handling high volumes of enterprise security questionnaires. Sprinto is best for: APAC-based SaaS companies, Indian startups seeking cost-effective compliance, and companies wanting a simple platform with essential automation.

## FAQ

### Which platform has better ISO 27001 support?

Both platforms support ISO 27001 alongside SOC 2. Spire's AI audit agent provides specific value for ISO 27001 ISMS documentation review. Sprinto offers good ISO 27001 coverage for APAC-based companies.

### Can either platform handle multi-entity compliance?

Spire supports multi-entity and multi-framework compliance for companies with multiple products or subsidiaries. Sprinto offers multi-framework support with a focus on SOC 2 and ISO 27001.

### Which platform is better for US enterprise sales?

For US enterprise sales, SOC 2 Type II coverage is the primary requirement. Both platforms provide SOC 2 support. Spire's AI questionnaire response automation provides additional value for US enterprise sales cycles that involve extensive security reviews.
