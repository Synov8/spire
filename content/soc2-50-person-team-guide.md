A 50-person company managing SOC 2 across multiple trust service criteria should allocate a dedicated compliance manager (full-time) supported by 2 to 3 part-time control owners across engineering, legal, and operations — totaling approximately 60 to 80 person-hours per month on compliance activities according to a 2025 IANS Research maturity model. At this scale, compliance shifts from a project to an operational program.

## What Compliance Team Structure Works at 50 People?

The dedicated compliance manager owns the SOC 2 program, auditor relationship, evidence management, and compliance reporting. A security engineer (half-time) owns technical evidence automation, control monitoring, and remediation tracking. A legal or privacy counsel (quarter-time) supports Privacy TSC, vendor contracts, and regulatory alignment. An executive sponsor (quarter-time) provides governance oversight and board-level reporting.

## What Multi-Framework Strategy Works at 50 People?

Companies with 50 employees typically maintain SOC 2 as their primary framework and add a secondary framework — ISO 27001 (most common), HIPAA (for health tech), or GDPR compliance program (for EU data processors). Design controls for framework portability from the start: one control implementation that satisfies multiple framework requirements.

## How Does Access Management Scale at 50 People?

At 50 employees, implement automated user provisioning and deprovisioning through your identity provider, quarterly access reviews covering all production systems, and role-based access control with standardized permission groups. The access review process at 50 people covers 40 to 60 users and should take 1 to 2 weeks per review cycle.

## FAQ

### What is the annual SOC 2 budget for a 50-person company?

$80,000 to $180,000 annually including audit ($30,000 to $55,000), compliance platform ($18,000 to $30,000), penetration testing ($12,000 to $20,000), compliance staff ($30,000 to $80,000 in allocated salary), and security tooling ($15,000 to $30,000).

### How many controls does a 50-person company need?

With Security + Availability scope: 80 to 110 controls. With multiple TSC: 110 to 150 controls. Automated evidence target: 75% to 85%.

### Should a 50-person company have a formal compliance committee?

Yes. A formal compliance committee meeting quarterly with defined members (compliance manager, CISO or VP Engineering, legal counsel, CFO or COO) provides governance structure that auditors view favorably.
